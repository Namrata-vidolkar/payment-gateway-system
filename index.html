Cyclones are associated with high-pressure gradients and consequent very strong winds damage installations, dwellings, communication systems, trees., etc.

Resulting in loss of life and property and prolonged rains due to cyclone cause river floods and submergence of low lying areas by rain causing loss of life and property.

Floods and coastal inundation due to storm surges pollute drinking water sources causing outbreak of epidemics.
